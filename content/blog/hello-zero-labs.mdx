# Hello Zero Labs: Building Investor-Ready AI Customer Support Automation

Zero Labs AI is focused on one outcome: dependable customer communication at scale. We help teams automate messaging and voice support without compromising brand trust, compliance, or the human experience.

## Evidence from the field

Modern customer support is under pressure to move faster while maintaining quality. We anchor our product roadmap in real-world evidence:

- In a large field experiment in a live call center, generative AI assistance improved productivity on average and delivered the biggest gains for less-experienced agents, while also improving customer sentiment and retention. (Brynjolfsson, Li, and Raymond, 2023)
- A 2024 customer survey found that consumers are most comfortable with AI-driven customer support when it clearly understands and resolves their questions, and that confidence increases when fast escalation to a human is available. (KPMG, 2024)

These results validate a simple principle: automation must be both efficient and trustworthy to scale.

## Why retrieval and grounding matter

Customer support automation cannot rely on static prompts alone. The most effective systems are retrieval-augmented: they fetch trusted knowledge and generate answers grounded in that data. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) formalizes this approach, improving performance on knowledge-intensive tasks by combining retrieval with generative modeling. (Lewis et al., 2020)

## Why hallucinations must be addressed

Large language models can produce confident but incorrect outputs. A recent survey on hallucinations in LLMs outlines the types of hallucinations, their causes, and the mitigation strategies that are required for production-grade systems. (A Survey on Hallucination in Large Language Models, 2023)

For investor-ready automation, grounding and verification are non-negotiable.

## The Zero Labs approach

We build customer support automation on three pillars:

1. **Grounded intelligence** with retrieval-aware workflows and curated knowledge bases.
2. **Operational visibility** with analytics and review trails across every automated interaction.
3. **Human-in-the-loop control** to ensure safe escalation and continuous improvement.

## What comes next

We will continue sharing field results, customer outcomes, and platform updates. Our goal is to help organizations move from experimental AI to reliable, enterprise-grade support automation.
