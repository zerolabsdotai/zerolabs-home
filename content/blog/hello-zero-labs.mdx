# Hello Zero Labs

We are building ZeroFlow to make operational knowledge flow like code. This is our first note on why now and why us.

## Why now

- The cost of reliable AI systems is rising.
- Teams are shipping more agents across more surfaces.
- Failure modes compound without shared operational context.

## What we are building

ZeroFlow is the control plane for AI operations: a shared map of systems, policies, and runbooks that stays in sync with reality.

### The first capabilities

- Live system maps that stay updated from telemetry.
- Playbooks tied to incidents, not folders.
- Reviewable changes with owners, history, and approvals.

## How we think about reliability

1. Make the system legible.
2. Make the response repeatable.
3. Make improvements measurable.

## What comes next

Over the next few weeks we will share product previews, early design decisions, and field lessons from pilot customers.
